1. Brand Overview

Mira is an AI-powered Instagram DM sales assistant built in Kenya for social-first shops. Mira handles product questions, recommendations, and orders so customers get fast, accurate answers — without the shop owner glued to their phone.

Mission

Help social-first shops serve customers quickly and easily, no matter how busy the day gets.

Target Audience

Kenyan SME shop owners who sell primarily through Instagram DMs. Solopreneurs and small teams who do everything themselves.

Brand Values

Empowering — the user is the business owner in control. Direct — say what it does, simply. Locally rooted — made in Kenya, priced in KES. Approachable — no jargon, no corporate speak.

2. Logo

The Mira logo is a clean wordmark in Bricolage Grotesque Bold. The app icon / favicon uses a lime-green rounded square with a white "M" letterform.

Clear Space & Minimum Size

Maintain clear space around the logo equal to the height of the "M" letterform. Minimum sizes: Digital — 120px width (wordmark), 40px (icon only). Print — 1 inch (wordmark), 0.25 inch (icon).

Logo Usage Rules

Do
Use approved color variants only
Maintain clear space
Use SVG where possible
Keep proportions locked
Don't
Rotate or skew the logo
Add shadows or gradients
Change the logo colors
Place on busy backgrounds

5. Voice & Tone

Brand Personality

Empowering but not patronizing. Direct but not blunt. Locally rooted but not provincial. Approachable but not casual.

Writing Guidelines

PrincipleExample
Use active voice"Mira answers questions" not "Questions are answered by Mira"
Address the user directly"Your shop" and "you" — never "our users"
Be specific"Handles Instagram DMs" not "Manages communications"
Avoid jargon"Sales assistant" not "conversational commerce solution"
Reference local contextKES pricing, M-Pesa, Instagram shops
