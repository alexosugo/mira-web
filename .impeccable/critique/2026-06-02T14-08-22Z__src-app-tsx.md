## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**AI slop: mostly no.** Per section: ProblemStatement mild (still a 2x2 icon-card grid, though calm); SolutionOverview clean; HowItWorks clean (numbers earned by a real sequence); Benefits borderline (the "list not grid" variation reuses the identical icon chip, so it reads as cards-minus-border); Pricing clean and disciplined; Footer mild (3 decorative lime touches). The page-level tell is no longer any one section but the ~12 repetitions of the same navy icon chip down the scroll.

**Deterministic scan**: static CLI 0; custom-class audit clean for all six sections. In-browser: 20 findings page-wide (incl. modal-open pass), in-scope verified real: **only 2 distinct patterns, both in the EliteContactModal** — thin-border + 50px-blur shadow chrome, and bounce easing (cubic-bezier 0.34,1.56,0.64,1) on the dialog entrance, plus transitive glass/glow/shimmer inherited from GlassButton. problem-statement, how-it-works, benefits, footer: **zero findings**. All gray-on-color flags are the known navy-100-on-navy FP family (12.49:1); gradient-text and "theater" flags were detector self-matches not present in page source.

## Overall Impression

The rest of the page now reads as the same product as the hero and features. Voice, tokens, signal discipline all hold. What remains: one positioning leak at the conversion moment (the roadmap line invents TikTok), the modal as the last unreworked surface, and a middle that may now be calm to a fault.

## What's Working

1. **Signal discipline is textbook**: lime appears only at Mira-is-on/do-this-next moments (hero CTA, Pro accent + badge, modal active states, success check). Whole middle lime-free.
2. **Copy is on-voice and specific** (verified: no exclamation points, em dashes, or buzzwords in scope).
3. **Verified hygiene**: no AA failures (floor 7.03:1), zero overflow at 320/390, reduced motion works, modal focus trap + Escape + backdrop + focus restore all pass in-browser; all three pricing CTAs read as buttons in both themes.

## Priority Issues

- **[P1] Roadmap line at the conversion moment invents a channel.** Pricing's "Coming soon on every plan: WhatsApp and TikTok." PRODUCT.md treats WhatsApp as roadmap-not-pitch and never mentions TikTok. Multi-channel framing resurfaces exactly at the buy moment. **Fix**: cut TikTok; move/soften the WhatsApp mention out of the decision zone or delete the line. **Suggested command**: /impeccable clarify.
- **[P2] Modal success state drops keyboard focus to body** (browser-verified): the focused submit button unmounts on success, nothing re-focuses, and AT never hears the confirmation. **Fix**: focus the success heading (tabIndex -1) and add role="status". **Suggested command**: /impeccable harden.
- **[P2] Modal is the last unreworked surface**: always dark regardless of page theme; thin-border + wide-shadow chrome; bounce easing on entrance; transitive glass/glow/shimmer via GlassButton; placeholder-as-label fields (DESIGN.md specifies floating labels); "Company Name / business goals" reads corporate to a solo seller. **Fix**: align the modal to the reworked system. **Suggested command**: /impeccable polish.
- **[P3] Icon-chip monotony**: the same 48px navy chip appears ~12 times across Problem/Benefits/Features. **Fix**: give one section a different device (checkmark or number rhythm, or no chip). **Suggested command**: /impeccable layout.
- **[P3] Pricing footnotes inject uncertainty at the decision point** ("*price scales with how many customers you serve" / "*priced to fit your shop"). **Fix**: concrete range or tooltip. **Suggested command**: /impeccable clarify.
- **[P3] No privacy/safety reassurance near the connect-your-Instagram ask**; Privacy/Terms intentionally absent (no fake links: right call) but the trust load is unmet. **Fix**: one line near HowItWorks step 1 or Pricing ("Mira reads only your shop's DMs; disconnect anytime") until legal pages exist. **Suggested command**: /impeccable clarify.
