## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**AI slop: yes, in every in-scope section.** The page visibly splits at the rework boundary.

- **ProblemStatement**: identical 2x2 icon-card grid.
- **SolutionOverview**: eyebrow pill with banned Sparkles icon, animated gradient border, two blurred lime blobs, single-item feature array.
- **HowItWorks**: "3 Simple Steps" eyebrow (lime-on-white, measured **1.98:1**, verified fail), glass-card chrome x3 (verified 1px + 32px shadow), double lime per card (number circle + icon tile), Sparkles again.
- **Benefits**: second 4-up identical icon grid; near-duplicate of ProblemStatement.
- **Pricing**: ported-template copy ("Growth Tools", "Automate conversations", "Optimize", "contacts", "No credit card, or charge card, required!"), full solid-lime Pro card, Free card with no dark variant, decorative radial blobs.
- **Footer**: standard 3-column; pulsing "Made in Kenya" dot (Glow rule violation); dead social/Privacy/Terms links.

**Deterministic scan**: static CLI 0 (sanity-checked working; it keys on AI-palette tells, not this chrome). In-browser: 36 findings, 29 in scope. Verified real: HowItWorks 1.98:1 lime badge, glass-card chrome x3, icon-tile stacks x7 (HowItWorks 3 + Benefits 4), global bounce easing. Verified false positives: all 8 Benefits "1.3:1/2.6:1" contrast findings (card backgrounds composite to white at 5% alpha; real ratios 7.56:1 and 15.56:1), Pricing/Footer/Solution clip findings (intentional blob clipping), Pricing gray-on-navy (measured 10.56:1 and 6.13:1, passing).

## Overall Impression

The middle of the page is three consecutive sections of telling (Problem, Solution, HowItWorks) between two sections of showing (Hero, Features), and the money moment switches voice entirely to ported SaaS-template copy. The single biggest opportunity: make Problem-to-Pricing sound and look like the same product that built the two DM demos.

## What's Working

1. **JS-off degradation is excellent** (browser-verified): clean noscript fallback with working app link.
2. **Reduced motion handled globally**, including the fill-mode/delay trap. (browser-verified)
3. **No horizontal overflow at 320/390**; pricing cards stack cleanly; all body text passes AA in both themes (the worst real reading was decorative lime, not body copy). (browser-verified)

## Priority Issues

- **[P0] Multi-channel copy contradicts the Instagram-DM positioning, twice.** ProblemStatement.tsx:18 ("Too Many Channels / WhatsApp here, Instagram there") and Pricing.tsx:31-48 (per-tier "WhatsApp (Coming Soon)" / "TikTok (Coming Soon)" channel lists). The first problem statement and the money moment both sell the wrong product. **Fix**: rewrite the Problem card to an IG-specific pain; collapse channel lists to one quiet "Coming soon" line. **Suggested command**: /impeccable clarify.
- **[P1] Pricing copy is ported-template SaaS slop with voice violations.** "Automate conversations", "Acquire new customers", "Growth Tools", "Optimize performance", "contacts", "FREE OF CHARGE", "No credit card, or charge card, required!" (exclamation). Lands at peak skepticism. **Fix**: rewrite in Mira's voice around what a seller gets. **Suggested command**: /impeccable clarify.
- **[P1] Solid-lime Pro card blows the One Signal Rule.** A third of the pricing grid is lime fill. **Fix**: navy highlighted card with lime border/badge; lime fill reserved for the primary CTA. **Suggested command**: /impeccable quieter.
- **[P1] Pricing Free card and comparison table have no dark-mode variants** (Pricing.tsx:96-117): pure white block on the navy page at night, the literal use case. **Fix**: add dark: variants matching Elite. **Suggested command**: /impeccable polish.
- **[P1] EliteContactModal has no focus trap** (browser-verified: Tab escapes to footer links behind the dialog; aria-modal set but unenforced). Also console.log of form PII at line 98 and "Thank you!" exclamation at 172. **Fix**: trap Tab/Shift+Tab, restore focus on close, delete the console.log. **Suggested command**: /impeccable harden.
- **[P2] SolutionOverview renders washed-out gray in light theme** (browser-verified): animated gradient border at opacity-20 blur-sm over the navy card desaturates it; plus two decorative lime blobs. **Fix**: solid navy surface like the Features lead card; delete border animation and blobs. **Suggested command**: /impeccable quieter.
- **[P2] Surviving eyebrow pills + Sparkles motif**: SolutionOverview:38-41, HowItWorks:36-38 (the 1.98:1 lime-on-white), Sparkles at HowItWorks:11. **Fix**: delete eyebrows; concrete icon for step 2. **Suggested command**: /impeccable quieter.
- **[P2] Dead links in Footer**: social href="#", Privacy/Terms buttons that only track. Trust-eroding for a hand-over-your-DMs ask. **Fix**: real URLs or remove until they exist. **Suggested command**: /impeccable harden.
- **[P2] Footer pulsing dot** violates Glow-Earns-Its-Keep. **Fix**: static dot.
- **[P3] ProblemStatement and Benefits are near-duplicate icon grids**; the page reads as the same module twice. **Fix**: differentiate one (outcome strip, paired visual, or lead-card hierarchy like Features). **Suggested command**: /impeccable layout.

## Questions to Consider

1. If Principle #1 is "show the DM," why do three consecutive sections (Problem, Solution, HowItWorks) contain zero DM? Could Problem + Solution collapse into one annotated DM screenshot?
